About the Author

Chidi E. O. Ezeobi was born in Nigeria and received his early education there. He attended Aguata High School, Anambra State, before migrating to the United States in 1996. He earned an AAS in Business Administration from Kingsborough Community College and a BSc in Professional Management from Medgar Evers College, New York.

He currently resides in Newark, New Jersey, and continues to contribute to literature through storytelling and published works.
